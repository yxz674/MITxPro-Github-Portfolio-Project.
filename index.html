PacMen Exercise

Pac-Man is a Japanese video game.The player guides the titular Pac-Man through a maze to eat Pac-Dots. The exercise here is to create PacMen with the click of a button and each of them will move about the browser window at their own pace.

Eye Exercise

Eye exercise is for practicing to create a web page with two eyes. Two eyes should be synchronized and both follow the mouse movement. As player move the mouse, the mouse events are being picked up and eyeballs are being moved to match the mouse.

Real Time Bus Tracker Exercise

In this exercise,an API open-source platform called mapbox is being used to source real-time data of the Boston MBTA busses. The MBTA bus data will determine stops between MIT and Harvard, and display the live location of the busses on a map.
